Recipe #1 Aglio e Olio

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta Noodles 100g
Garlic, minced 6 cloves
Olive Oil 20g
Chilli Peppers 5g
Salt Slightly
Black Pepper Slightly

Step 1: Bring a large pot of cold water to a boil over high heat, then salt it generously.
Add the pasta and cook, stirring occasionally.

Step 2: While the pasta cooks, combine the garlic, olive oil in a large skillet and warm over low heat, stirring occasionally, until the garlic softens and turns golden.

Step 3: Drain the pasta in a colander set in the sink, reserving about 5 spoons of the cooking water. Add the pasta and the reserved water to the garlic mixture. Add salt and black pepper. Mix well.

Step 4: Transfer to a large serving bowl.

Recipe #2 Salmon Donburi

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Steamed Rice 1 serving
Sliced Salmon 6 pcs
Sliced Onions 1/4 cups
Soy Sauce 3 spoons
Wasabi 1/2 spoons

Step 1: Serve steamed rice in a bowl, let it cool off slightly with soy sauce.

Step 2: Assemble sliced salmon (sashimi), wasabi and other toppings that you add on top.

Recipe #3 Korean Corn Cheese

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Fresh corn 3 spoons
Mayonnaise 3 spoons
Shredded Mozzarella
Sugar 3 spoons

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400F.

Step 2: Combine all the ingredients into a mixing bowl and stir together.Season with salt and pepper and give it one more good stir.

Step 3: Pour mixture into a lightly greased baking dish.

Step 4: Bake corn cheese for 10 minutes to melt the cheese and heat through.

Step 5: Transfer the dish into the broiler and broil for about 2 minutes to make it bubbly and brown on top.
